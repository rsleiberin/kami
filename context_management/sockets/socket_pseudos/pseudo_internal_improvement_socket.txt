InternalImprovementSocket:

Handles the detection of internal improvements and takes relevant actions to optimize the AGI system.

class InternalImprovementSocket:

    - Initialization:
        Set up attributes to track detected improvements and actions taken.

    - detect_improvements():
        Periodically or based on triggers, scan the system for potential areas of improvement and validate them.

    - scan_for_improvements():
        Use metrics, logs, and other data to identify potential areas of optimization or enhancement.

    - is_valid_improvement(improvement):
        Validate if a detected improvement is actionable and beneficial.

    - take_action():
        Act on the detected improvements.

    - determine_action(improvement):
        Decide on the best course of action based on the nature of the detected improvement.

    - execute_action(action):
        Implement the determined action.
